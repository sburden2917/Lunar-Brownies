Lunar Brownies Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Lunar Brownies application ("Lunar Brownies," "the App") handles information. Lunar Brownies is a personal automation tool operated by an individual creator and is not a commercial service.

Information We Access

When you authorize Lunar Brownies through TikTok Login, the App receives an access token and your basic public profile (display name and avatar) solely to confirm the correct account is connected. Lunar Brownies does not collect, store, or process any other personal data.

How Information Is Used

The access token is used exclusively to upload and publish video content to the authenticated user's own TikTok account via TikTok's Content Posting API. Lunar Brownies acts only on behalf of the account owner who authorized it.

Data Storage and Retention

Authentication tokens are stored locally within the owner's private automation environment and are used only to maintain the connection to TikTok. No user data is shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party. Tokens can be revoked at any time from your TikTok account settings, which immediately ends the App's access.

Content

All video content published through Lunar Brownies is AI-generated and is labeled as AI-generated content in accordance with TikTok's synthetic-media policy.

Third-Party Services

Lunar Brownies interacts only with TikTok's official APIs. Your use of TikTok remains governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the app owner at the email registered with this application's TikTok developer account.
