Stories, forged into video.

Tale Forge turns written stories into fully narrated, illustrated videos — chapter by chapter, scene by scene — and publishes them to your own connected social accounts on a schedule.

What is Tale Forge?

Tale Forge is a personal content-automation studio. You start with a story outline; Tale Forge writes it out, generates an illustration and an AI voice narration for every scene, stitches the scenes into chapter videos and short vertical clips, then publishes them to the social account you've connected — including TikTok via the Content Posting API. It is operated by an individual creator for their own channels.

Responsible AI & content

Every video Tale Forge publishes is AI-generated and is flagged as AI-generated content (the is_aigc flag) when posted, in accordance with TikTok's synthetic-media policy. Tale Forge posts only to accounts whose owners have explicitly authorized it through official TikTok Login, and acts solely on that owner's behalf.

Contact

Tale Forge is operated by an individual creator. For questions about the app, its data handling, or these policies, contact the owner at the email registered with this application's TikTok developer account.
